Our Story

Founded in 2018, Alte began as a small woodworking shop with a passion for quality craftsmanship. What started as a solo endeavor quickly grew into a community of artisans, designers, and builders united by a shared vision.

Today, we're proud to have completed over 200 projects that not only showcase beautiful design but also foster connection and community. From custom furniture to complete interior transformations, every Alte project tells a story.

Our team of skilled craftsmen and designers work closely with clients to create spaces that are both functional and inspiring. We believe that good design should bring people together and enhance everyday life.
